About Me

My name is Courtney Kusmierz. My nickname is Kusco. I have no idea why, but my friends made it up and have been calling me that ever since. My hobbies include hiking, kayaking, camping, going mudding, and horseback riding. Every year, my dad and I try to go to Gatlinburg to hike the Smoky Mountains to get up to the cliff top and Leconte Lodge.

Hobbies

We have friends in Missouri who have horses. I got bucked off one of their horses and hurt my shoulder. My dad broke his foot and got a gash on his face from being bucked off of one. We also kayak with them. When we go kayaking, we are usually on the water for 8 hours. Then we camp out on the banks of the river and keep going the next day.

Overview

Hiking, camping, and kayaking are my main hobbies. These are things that I have been doing for as long as I can remember. These hobbies are more than just hobbies to me, they are a part of me and my story.
